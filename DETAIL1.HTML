DETAILS ABOUT STRETCHINGS

Benefits of stretching:

- Improved Flexibility: Regular stretching increases your range of motion, making everyday activities easier.
- Reduced Injury Risk: Prepares muscles for exercise, reducing the likelihood of strains and sprains.
- Enhanced Performance: Improves flexibility and range of motion, boosting overall workout performance.
- Better Posture: Corrects muscle imbalances and improves posture.
- Relaxation and Stress Relief: Promotes relaxation and reduces stress.
